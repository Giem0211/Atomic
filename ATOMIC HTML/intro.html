LESSON 1: Intermolecular Forces (IMF)

This module is about general chemistry 2, Intermolecular Forces (IMF), lesson 1 in quarter 3 that will serve as the learning material of the students for a week. After going through this module, the students are expected to:

  1. Use the kinetic molecular model to explain properties of liquids and solids.
  2. Describe and differentiate the types of intermolecular forces

LESSON 2: Properties of liquids and Intermolecular Forces (IMF)

This module is about general chemistry 2, Properties of liquids and Intermolecular Forces (IMF), lesson 2 in quarter 3 that will serve as the learning material of the students for a week. After going through this module, the students are expected to:

  1. Describe the following properties of liquids and explain the effect of intermolecular forces on these properties: surface tension, viscosity, vapor pressure, boiling point, and molar heat of vaporization.
  2. DExplain the properties of water with its molecular structure and intermolecular forces.
