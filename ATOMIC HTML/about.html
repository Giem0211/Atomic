About Project ATOMIC

Project ATOMIC: Application for Terse Online Material In Chemistry is created as a learning application platform that will help teachers and students interact with lessons in General Chemistry 2. This application is created by Senior High Students from Luis Palad Integrated High School as output for their Capstone Project.
